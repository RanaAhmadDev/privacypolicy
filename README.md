Privacy Policy — Farm Guide

Last updated: June 5, 2026

1. Introduction

Farm Guide (the "App") helps farmers manage livestock, crops, and farm records, provides an AI assistant and a disease-scanner feature. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and the choices you have. The policy is specific to the App and its features.

2. Information We Collect

- Information You Provide Directly: When you register or edit your profile we collect name, email address, phone number, farm name, and profile photo. You may also provide farm records, animals data, and other agricultural information when using the App's features.
- Photos & Media: If you use the disease scanner or upload photos (profile or reports), the images are uploaded to our backend and may be used to provide scanning results and to improve the scanner model (with appropriate protections).
- Usage & Device Data: We collect device info and usage data (OS version, device model, app version, session timestamps, feature usage, crash logs) to operate and improve the App.
- AI Logs: Interactions with the AI Assistant (text prompts, responses) may be logged temporarily for quality monitoring and model improvement, anonymized where feasible.
- Payment & Billing Data: If you purchase subscriptions or in-app services, payment is processed by the app store or a third-party payment provider. We do not store full payment card details on our servers; only transaction references and billing metadata needed to manage your subscription.

3. How We Use Your Information

- To provide core app functionality: profile, farm records, disease scanner, AI assistant, syncing, and notifications.
- To process and validate transactions and subscriptions via third-party payment processors.
- To improve the App: analytics, crash reporting, diagnostics, and AI model improvements.
- To communicate: respond to support requests, send important account or service messages.
- To comply with legal obligations and protect rights, safety, or property.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

- Service providers: hosting (Firebase / Google Cloud), analytics (Google Analytics / Firebase Analytics), crash reporting (Sentry / Crashlytics), payment processors, and other vendors who process data on our behalf.
- Legal & safety: law enforcement or regulators when required by law.

These providers may process data in other countries; please consult their privacy policies for details.

5. Images and AI Processing

- Disease Scanner: Images you submit for disease scanning are processed to generate results. Images may be stored in our backend to allow reprocessing, improve detection models, and support your support requests. If you do not want your images used for model improvement, contact us (see Section 11).
- AI Assistant: Text inputs and short-term logs may be retained for improving the assistant. We remove personal identifiers where reasonable during model training.

6. Payments and Financial Data

- In-app purchases and subscriptions are handled by the platform store (Google Play / App Store) or a third-party payment provider. We only keep transaction metadata (order id, subscription status, plan) needed to manage your account. We do not store full payment card numbers on our servers.

7. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide services and meet legal obligations. You can request deletion of your account and personal data (see Section 11). Some aggregated or anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for analytics.

8. Security

We use reasonable organizational and technical safeguards (secure hosting, encrypted transport, access controls) to protect your data. No system is perfectly secure — if you believe your account has been compromised, contact us immediately.

9. Your Rights & Choices

- Access & Correction: You can view and edit your profile data inside the App.
- Deletion: To delete your account and personal data, contact us at the address below and we will guide you through the process.
- Opt-outs: You can opt out of non-essential analytics where supported in App settings.

10. Children

The App is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover such data, we will delete it promptly.

11. Contact & Requests

For privacy requests (access, deletion, data export) or questions, contact:

support@farmguide.app

If you need to escalate, include your account email and a clear request so we can verify and process it.

12. International Transfers

Data may be processed or stored in countries other than your own (for example, via cloud providers). By using the App you consent to these transfers.

13. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy to reflect app changes. We will post the updated version with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use after changes indicates acceptance.
